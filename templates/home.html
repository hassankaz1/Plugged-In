How to Search!

Enter your location. Sorry as of now this site is restricted to USA only due to API costs.

Search results will only display places and not home addresses. So search for a store or airport near you for best results. For more accurate results you may also use your current location through the current location button.

Then enter the charging port you want to filter your search with or keep all options open.

And wala you have a list of the chargers near you. If there are any reviews left you may also view them.
